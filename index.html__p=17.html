Visa Information

The CSWIM2014 Organizing Committee can issue an invitation letter ( in support of a Chinese visa application ) to our registrants. Please (1) download the template, (2) fill in your information, and (3) send the completed file with your request to yuanhua@uestc.edu.cn for the official invitation letter.
