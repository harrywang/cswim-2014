The 8th China Summer Workshop on Information Management (CSWIM 2014) will be held in Chengdu, China on June 21-22, 2014. It will be hosted by the School of Management and Economics at University of Electronic Science and Technology of China. The purpose of the workshop is to provide a forum for researchers, practitioners and developers where they can share views, present ideas and original work, providing new insights into information management-related areas. The theme of CSWIM 2014 is “Digital Innovation and Transformation.”

We welcome submissions of original research papers addressing issues concerning the theory, design, development, evaluation, and application of information systems and management. You are invited to submit papers on the information management-related topics of interest.  For a list of topics of interest and submission instructions, see Call for Papers.

We look forward to seeing you at CSWIM 2014 in Chengdu, China. Please check this website often for updates.
