VENUE & ACCOMMODATIONS

Venue

The conference venue is the First Teaching Building located in the Shahe Campus of UESTC,  (Add: No.4, Section 2, North Jianshe  Road, Chengdu)

Map for Shahe Campus of UESTC.

From Shuangliu International Airport (A) to Shahe Campus of UESTC (B)  (About 25 km / 15 mile).
